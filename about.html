About Neckass

Neckass is a privacy-first, browser-first headline generator for humorous, absurd news-style lines. It runs as a static website with no account requirement and no backend dependency.

The app combines curated headline material with optional lightweight local generation features so you can quickly create, copy, share, and export social-ready joke headlines.

What you can do

  • Shuffle through funny headlines instantly.
  • Copy a headline to your clipboard in one click.
  • Share directly with native share and social links.
  • Export a mock front page image as PNG.
